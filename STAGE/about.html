Get to Know Me

A native of Marin. A trusted advocate.

As a native of Marin County, having lived, worked and volunteered within the Bay Area all of my life, I understand the area, its varied communities and complex markets intimately. I have been a licensed real estate agent since 1999. That combined with my over 25 years in business, banking and real estate finance makes me uniquely qualified to represent you with confidence in one of the biggest transactions of your life.

The attributes and qualities that I bring yield exponential results for my clients. I have a strong, decades-long background in sales and marketing with deep roots and countless connections in this community. I continually conduct extensive research of the markets to maintain my expertise and inform my clients of trends.

I thrive when engaged in frank and honest, productive negotiations, which ultimately leads to smooth transactions for my clients. I utilize the latest technology in addition to engaging my vast professional and personal networks, as I strategize together with you to prepare your property for sale for the right price at the best possible time.
